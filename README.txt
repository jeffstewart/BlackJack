This program calculates the relative probabilities of busting in blackjack.

It keeps track of the cards that are left in the shoe to give more accurate probabilities.
Helps players find their best option in a given situation.

Keeps track of the count to help players to know when to bet high.

Does not give an option for splitting or doubling.
Probability of dealer busting only calculates the prob that the dealer will hit once and bust on his third card.
Does not calculate prob that dealer will have to hit again.

The goal is to determine how much discrepancy there is between basic strategy and the actual odds as a game progresses
and cards are taken out of play.
